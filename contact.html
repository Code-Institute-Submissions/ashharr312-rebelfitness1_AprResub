About Us!

Rebel Fitness was founded in 2017 with just a team of 4 but has grown exponentially to over 20! We wanted our gym to be a community where fitness and wellness is celebrated with a cool community vibe and support system. We strive everyday to improve upon the services that we provide.We want to help you reach your health and fitness goals while also making a few friends along the way! Our facilities are second to none with 3 floors, 1 resistance training floor, 1 functional zone floor and 1 cardio floor as well as 2 fitness studios for a variety of classes! We are stocked with the latest of gym equipment and also have our own shop!
